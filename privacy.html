🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026. Complete zero-telemetry client sandbox architecture statement.

1. Local Processing Architecture

At SolidColor, we value your creative assets. All digital assets, canvas setups, text hex entries, and film grain rendering operations run entirely inside your web browser's local sandbox environment. No active graphic data, image layers, or specific measurement numbers are ever transmitted across external cloud servers or tracking architectures.

2. Zero Data Collection Metrics

We do not use trackers, background account frameworks, or internal databases to monitor your design files. Your canvas exports remain 100% your exclusive, untracked creative property.

3. Third-Party Advertising Disclosures

To support our free creator utility operations, we utilize verified advertising platforms like Google AdSense to serve programmatic ads when you use our workspace. These systems may use baseline cookies to present highly contextualized advertisements tailored to your web interests.
